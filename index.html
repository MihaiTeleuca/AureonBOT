Common questions

Is Aureon only for economy servers?

No. Economy and RPG systems are a big part of Aureon, but the bot also includes moderation, security, automations, tickets, activity and utility features for general communities.

Can the bot be used in different languages?

Yes. The website language selector includes English, Romanian, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese and Hindi.

Does every server need all modules enabled?

No. Aureon is easier to present as a modular bot: servers can use the systems that fit their community and keep the rest disabled or reserved for later.
